Cerial Recipe For Beginners

Ready to make the best cerial in the world?

This receipe will give you all the skills you need to make your very own cerial every morning before working on your startup!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Your favorite brand of cerial
  • 1 bowl
  • 1 spoon (2 if you're sharing)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Place bowl on a table.
  2. Step 2: Put spoon(s) inside bowl
  3. Step 3: open cerial box and poor one serving into the bowl
  4. Step 4: Add a reasonalble amount of milk. Please do not drown your cerial
  5. Step 5: Grab spoon and dig in! Enjoy your cerial!
