How to Make a Refreshing Glass of Lemonade (For Beginners)

Ready to make the most refreshing lemonade ever?

This recipe will guide you through creating your very own tangy and sweet glass of lemonade. Perfect for a hot summer day!

Ingredients

  • 4-6 fresh lemons
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 6 cups of water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Roll the lemons on a hard surface to soften them, then cut them in half.
  2. Step 2: Juice the lemons using a citrus juicer or by hand, removing any seeds.
  3. Step 3: In a pitcher, mix the freshly squeezed lemon juice with sugar and stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Step 4: Add water to the pitcher and stir well to combine.
  5. Step 5: Taste the lemonade and adjust the sweetness or tartness by adding more sugar or lemon juice as needed.
  6. Step 6: Refrigerate the lemonade for at least 30 minutes to chill.
  7. Step 7: When ready to serve, fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the chilled lemonade over the ice.
  8. Step 8: Garnish with fresh mint leaves, if desired.
  9. Step 9: Stir well and enjoy your refreshing glass of homemade lemonade!
